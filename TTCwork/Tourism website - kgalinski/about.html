Who we are.

Łodz tourism is a small company based in the heart of Łodz right next door to Manufaktura. We specialise in arranging trips around the city where we try to cover as much of its history as we can.

What we do.

In partnership with local businesses we arrange daily outings around the city where we visit historic sites and try to educate tourists on their incredible stories in hopes to preserve it for future generations.
